The African continent is home to many innovative tech startups that are driving change and economic growth.
AfriTech features profiles of these startups, interviews with their founders, and news on the latest developments
in the African tech scene. We also focus on emerging technologies that are gaining traction in Africa, such as fintech,

Ultimately, the key of AfriTech is to focus on what makes Africa unique and to provide content and resources that

Africans Building Africa

Africans Building Africa is a powerful statement that reflects the growing movement of African entrepreneurs,
innovators, and change-makers who are creating new opportunities and driving economic growth across the continent.
This movement is fueled by a strong sense of pride in African culture and heritage, a belief in the power of innovation
and creativity, and a commitment to creating a better future for all Africans. By building Africa, Africans are taking ownership
of their own destinies and shaping their own futures. They are leveraging technology and other resources to solve some of the
continent's most pressing challenges, such as access to healthcare, education, and financial services. They are creating jobs and
economic opportunities, empowering local communities, and building a brighter future for generations to come. This movement
is not just about individual success stories, but about creating a collective impact that benefits all Africans. By building Africa,
Africans are breaking down barriers, challenging stereotypes, and reshaping the narrative around the continent. They are showing
the world that Africa is not just a place of poverty and conflict, but a land of innovation, creativity, and limitless potential. In short,
Africans Building Africa is a powerful symbol of hope and possibility. It is a call to action for all Africans to embrace their potential,
to take ownership of their futures, and to work together to build a continent that is inclusive, prosperous, and sustainable for all.

Top 5 African Tech Startups to Watch

  • Andela

    Andela is a technology company that helps companies build high-performing engineering teams by investing in Africa's most talented software developers.

  • Flutterwave

    Flutterwave is a payment technology company that provides seamless and secure payment solutions for businesses and individuals across Africa.

  • Lori Systems

    Lori Systems is a logistics platform that connects cargo owners with transporters to provide reliable and affordable transportation services across Africa.

  • M-Farm

    M-Farm is a mobile app that connects smallholder farmers in Kenya with markets and other resources, helping them to increase their profits and improve their livelihoods.

  • uLesson

    uLesson is an edtech platform that provides interactive and engaging video lessons, quizzes, and practice tests for high school students across Africa.
